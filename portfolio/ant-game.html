Ant Game

What is Ant Game?

Ant Game is a game about an ordinary man who is going about his business when he suddenly gets attacked by ants. These ants ruin his garden, steal his food, and just create a huge mess. You, the player, decide to take revenge on these ants and want to eradicate their colony. So, you grab your special crossbow, reliable grappling hook, and decide to shrink yourself down to the size of an ant to fight back against the ant invasion!

Made for a HvA school project.
